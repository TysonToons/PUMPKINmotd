Welcome to P.U.M.P.K.I.N.
A multiplayer horror game with a Gorilla Tag based movement system!

Here, there are many things that lurk within the shadows, so be sure to use your trusty headlight!
It isn't all that bad though, because we have a safe place hidden from the monsters where you can buy/equip hats, costumes, masks or gloves!
We aren't sure if there are other places out there, but stay safe if you're thinking about finding them.
